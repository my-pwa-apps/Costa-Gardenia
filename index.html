    wb_sunny Costa Gardenia

    Visualize the sun's path at any location to evaluate natural light for your future home. Use the Map to set a location, Sun Path for a 3D visualization, and AR View to see the sun's trajectory overlaid on your camera view — including how nearby buildings may cast shadows.

    How to Use

    1. Search or click on the map to set your location.
    2. Switch to Sun Path to see the 3D arc for any date/time.
    3. Adjust the date and time sliders to explore different seasons.
    4. Open AR View on-site to overlay the sun path on your camera.
    5. Tap buildings in AR to mark obstructions and see shadow impact.

    Sun calculations are astronomically accurate within ~1°. Building obstruction analysis is approximate.
